Orbital CubeSat

CAD Drawing of Chassis

Key Points:

  • Designed the CubeSat bus (frame) and various mechanical systems such as a battery holder, and helped improve the misalignment of mounting holes on the bottom brace of the payload and updating the CAD model of the chassis for future competitions.
  • Conducted Finite Element Modeling (FEM) for all designed components to assess structural integrity and performance under launch and space conditions.
  • Achieved first place in the Canadian Satellite Design Challenge (CSDC-6) in 2023.
