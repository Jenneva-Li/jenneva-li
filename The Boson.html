What is The Boson?

The Boson is an upcoming customizable open source XR headset by Wire; Walker Industries Research and Experimentation, an R&D company based in Miami Gardens, Florida, United States.

Key Points:

  • Designing a new VR glove concept CAD model with multiple size options in SolidWorks, ensuring ergonomic fit, optimized component integration, and enhanced user comfort across all hand sizes.
  • Developed CAD models of an upcoming open-source XR (Extended Reality) headset, translating initial concept drawings into detailed designs utilized in pre-launch promotional campaigns.
